Welcome to Prince Andrew Sim (name pending)!
In this game, you are Prince Andrew himself, left alone to your devices in Buckingham Palace or whatever.
You have made the horrifying revelation that Dracula resides within and has a taste for your blood. Alas, you would surely seem insane if you told the public.
However, you have the power of the royal guard, the nation's best spies, as many Catholics as you can imagine, and taxes on your side.

Investigate to grow closer to finding dracula, hire guards to fend him off, hire priests to temporarily thwart him, and impose taxes to improve your income. Good luck.
